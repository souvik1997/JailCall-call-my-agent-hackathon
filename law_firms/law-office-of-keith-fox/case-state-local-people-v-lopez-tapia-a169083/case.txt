Description: Jamir Lopez-Tapia was convicted of one count of witness dissuasion and sentenced to the middle term of two years in state prison, doubled to four years by operation of a prior strike allegation. He argues that the trial court failed to comply with its obligation under Penal Code 1 section 1170, subdivision (b)(6) (section 1170(b)(6)) to apply a presumption in favor of the lower term based on his childhood trauma.
