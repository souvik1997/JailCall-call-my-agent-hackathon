ASH Legal offers flat-fee representation starting at $2,800 for most misdemeanor cases. DUI defense starts at $5,000. Fees get discussed upfront during your free consultation so there are no surprises — no hourly billing, no invoices you didn't expect. Do I really need a lawyer for a misdemeanor? Yes. Even a "minor" misdemeanor conviction shows up on background checks, affects employment, disqualifies you from housing, and can create immigration consequences. A lawyer can often get charges reduced or dismissed entirely. What happens at my first court date (arraignment)? At arraignment, the judge reads the charges and you enter a plea. I handle this for you — and in many misdemeanor cases, I can appear on your behalf so you don't have to miss work. Can my case be dismissed? Many misdemeanor cases in San Francisco are eligible for diversion programs, civil compromise, or dismissal in the interest of justice. During your consultation, I'll assess the specific options available for your charges. Will this show up on my background check? It depends on the outcome. A conviction will typically appear. A dismissal may still show as an arrest unless you take additional steps to seal the record. I advise every client on record-clearing options as part of the representation.
